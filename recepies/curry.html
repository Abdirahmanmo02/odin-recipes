Curry

a dish composed with a sauce or gravy seasoned with a mixture of ground spices that is thought to have originated in India and has since spread to many regions of the world.

Ingredients

  • onion
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • masala, curry powder
  • tomatoes
  • whipping cream
  • Chicken

Step by Step

  1. Brown the meat
  2. Sweat the onions
  3. Add the spices
  4. Simmer the curry
  5. Enjoy!
